The Best Movies According to Bagus

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Grave Of The Fireflies (1998)

The story can make me cry. This movie tells the story of a kid called Seita and Setsuko who became a victim of a world war in Japan

Sen to Chihiro No Kamikakushi / Spirited Away (2001)

This movie follows the adventure of Chihiro Ogino, a ten year old girl who is accidentally caught in the world of the spirit after her parents turned into pig by Yubaba's witches

Fate/Stay Night: Heavens Feel 3. Spring Song (2020)

This movie presents a perfect combination of thrilling fantasy and action. The story in this movie continued from the event in the previous film
